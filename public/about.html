About Take A Hike.


Going out into the woods alone can be a fun and exiting experience, but we all know that it can be rather dangerous out there on your own. Let's face it; it's safer and usually more fun to bring other hiking buddies along. But the problem with going in a group can be finding other like-minded individuals that can match your schedule, location and physical abilities.

Our aim is to connect potential nature enthusiasts with other like-minded people and provide a safer way to enjoy the outdoors together.

Life is a journey and we know that it's better with a friend. Or hundreds...
